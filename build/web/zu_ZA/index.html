Blokada, the ad blocker

If you want to efficiently block ads, trackers, malware, save on your data plan, speed up your device and protect your privacy with just one application, then Blokada is for you. It is free, secure and open source.

I-Blokada isebenza kuzo zonke izinhlelo zakho, hhayi i-inthanethi kuphela! It doesn't require root, supports both Wi-Fi and mobile networks, and is perfectly safe and simple to use. Vele uyivule, yona izosebenza ngokuthula!

Indaba yethu

Blokada was first released in November 2016, by the developer Karol Gusak. Umgomo kwakuwukugcina amadivaysi akhululekile futhi agweme okuqukethiwe okungadingeki okusebenzisa idatha yeselula ngokweqile. Izici zokuqala nezilungiso zithunyelwe masinyane, isihloko sohlelo kwiforamu elidumile le-XDA sasinezibuyekezo zehora. Ukuze itholakale kalula kubasebenzisi futhi ibasize kalula, iphrojekthi yavula indlela youxhumana kwi-Telegram. Amalunga amabili okuqala kwakunguKarol kanye nelinye ilunga lesigungu, uPéter Róth. Kamuva waqala ukuvuselela ikhasi le-Facebook ne-website yevimba sikhangiso, ngaphandle kwalokho waze wasiza ekamelweni lengxoxo elikhula ngokushesha.

Ukwakha iqembu eliza ukuhumusha uhlelo, lakhe okuqukethiwe, ukuhlola nokuguqula izinguqulo ze-beta nokuphatha amakhasi omphakathi, I-Blokada Insiders yasungulwa ngo-2018 futhi isebenza ngenkuthalo ukwenza i-Blokada ibe impumelelo eyiyona. Ubuwazi ukuthi manje i-Blokada itholakala ngezilimu ezingu-25?
Sebastian Januszevski joined the administration of the Telegram channel, assisting members and actively contributing towards testing the application, reproducing user reports. Ngokusekelwe kokukhathazeka kwakhe nolwazi, i-Blokada yaqina futhi yithembekile.

UKarol wazalwa e-Poland. Ngemva kokuchitha iminyaka eminingana eSweden, usebuyele eWarsaw, lapho ehlanganyela khona emisebenzini ehlukahlukene yeselula njengomthuthukisi. Awumangele, angithi?
UPéter uvela eHungary, esiseduze nenhloko-dolobha yezwe, iBudapest, lapho asebenza njengonjiniyela emkhakheni we-IT, ekwazi ukujula kobuchwepheshe be-Big Data, esekela izinkampani emhlabeni wonke ezaziwayo ukuze zifeze umgomo wazo.
Sebastian lives and works in Buenos Aires where he teaches statistics and data processing in a university. Ungumthuthukisi wokuzilibazisa ku-PHP ne-JavaScript, futhi kukhishwe ama-plugin ambalwa we-WordPress ngaphansi kwegama lakhe.

Since the beginning, Blokada was downloaded and installed on more than 200.000 devices, from phones to tablets to smart TVs.

Sithini isibonakalo sethu? Ithimba lizosebenza ukusekela ububanzi bamadivayisi, lenze kube lula ukuxhuma kunsizakalo yangempela ye-VPN ngenkathi i-Blokada isebenza futhi ivikela ubumfihlo bakho ngaphezulu, ikunike ulwazi oluningi mayelana nezikhangiso ozivikelwe kuzo.

Landa I-Blokada

First, you need to configure your device to allow installation of apps outside of Google Play. Chofoza lapha ukuze uthole isifundo esifushane sevidiyo. Bese uchofoza inkinobho engezansi:



Download Blokada v4.1


Landa I-Blokada namba-3.7 (egcinileyo kunamba-3)


Landa I-Blokada namba-3.4 (ezinzile kakhulu kunamba-3)

Landa I-Blokada namba-2 (ye-Android 4 kuya ezansi)



Sicela uqaphele: Inguqulo egcwele yohlelo, ngenxa yesimo sebhizinisi se-Google, ayivumelekile ku-Google Play. Hence, the version available on Play Store only allows changing the DNS service.

Eshintsha isevisi ye-DNS esuka kwistolo se-Google Play
