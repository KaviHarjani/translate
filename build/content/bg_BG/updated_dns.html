Blokada Slim v4.3

Вашата Blokada току-що се актуализира!

Има две издания на Blokada. Това, което използвате наличен в Google Play се нарича Blokada Slim (старо име: DNS changer от Blokada). This is not an adblocker. It gives you a possibility to change your DNS servers, and also to use our brand new VPN service (optional). The open source version of Blokada is available at our website, blokada.org.

You may have noticed the new UI in this update. This is the new v4 interface, and we hope you will like it! For more info about the changes check our changelog.

Remember that using the VPN or donating is what makes it possible for us to keep improving the app. Thanks!
